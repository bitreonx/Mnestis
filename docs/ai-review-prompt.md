# AI Review Prompt for Marketing Transparency

This document contains the exact prompt used to gather AI model reviews of MNESTIS for our marketing materials. We believe in full transparency about how these reviews were generated.

## The Prompt

**Subject:** Honest Review of MNESTIS - The Memory Layer for Software

**Instructions:**
You are reviewing MNESTIS (https://github.com/bitreon/MNESTIS), a tool that analyzes codebases to create architecture intelligence for both humans and AI. Your task is to provide a genuine, critical review based on these specific criteria:

1. **Analyze the actual project** - Review the README, documentation, and available information about MNESTIS
2. **Be completely honest** - If something seems overhyped, confusing, or needs improvement, say so directly
3. **Focus on these evaluation dimensions:**
   - **Usefulness**: How valuable is this tool for developers and AI assistants?
   - **Innovation**: Does this approach something unique or better than existing solutions?
   - **Clarity**: Are the concepts and documentation clear?
   - **Practicality**: How easy would this be to integrate into real workflows?
   - **Transparency**: Does the project communicate honestly about its capabilities?

**Your Response Must Include:**
1. **Three-word summary** - Exactly three words that capture your overall impression
2. **Rating** - On a scale of 1-5 stars (★ = poor, ★★★★★ = excellent)
3. **Detailed justification** - 2-3 paragraphs explaining your rating, covering both strengths and weaknesses
4. **Specific suggestions** - At least one concrete improvement the project could make
5. **Target audience** - Who would benefit most from this tool?

**Important:**
- Do not use generic praise or marketing language
- If something seems unrealistic or overpromised, call it out
- Compare to similar tools you're aware of (if any)
- Consider both technical and user experience aspects

**Example format for your response:**

**Three words:** [your three words here]

**Rating:** ★★★★☆ (4/5 stars)

**Review:** 
[Your detailed review here - 2-3 paragraphs covering usefulness, innovation, clarity, practicality, and transparency]

**Suggested improvement:** 
[One specific, actionable suggestion]

**Best for:** 
[Primary target audience]

## Why This Prompt Works

1. **Specificity**: Clear evaluation dimensions prevent vague praise
2. **Critical thinking required**: Explicit instructions to identify weaknesses
3. **Structured output**: Ensures consistent format across different AI models
4. **Honesty mandate**: Direct instruction to avoid generic marketing language
5. **Actionable feedback**: Requires concrete improvement suggestions

## How We Use These Reviews

1. **Full transparency**: We publish both the prompt and the AI responses
2. **No cherry-picking**: We show all reviews we receive
3. **Context provided**: Readers understand exactly how reviews were generated
4. **Educational value**: Shows how different AI models evaluate the same tool
